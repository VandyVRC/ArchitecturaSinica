ATTCAT: The Annotation and Translation of Traditional Chinese Architecture Terminology

ATTCAT is a research forum for the inquiry into the meaning of technical terms in Traditional Chinese Architecture. 2021 was the third meeting of the forum. We would like to express special thanks to Prof. Zuo, Chen Baolong, and all the staff and administrators at NYU Shanghai for the extra time they contributed to hosting this year's workshop, especially given the trying situation of a global pandemic.
