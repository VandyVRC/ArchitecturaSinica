ATTCAT The Annotation and Translation of Traditional Chinese Architecture Terminology

ATTCAT 2024 was the sixth meeting of the forum and we were happy to be back on the beautiful campus of Southeast University in Nanjing. We would like to express our appreciation to the faculty, students, and staff of SEU for their hard work in creating another amazing workshop for both in-person and online participants.
