柱頭鋪作 zhùtóu pūzuò

https://architecturasinica.org/keyword/k000120
Print   TEI/XML  

Terms

  • zhutou puzuo (Pinyin without tones)
  • 柱頭鋪作 (Traditional Chinese)
  • 柱头铺作 (Simplified Chinese)
  • zhùtóu pūzuò (Pinyin)
  • chu-t'ou p'u-tso (Wade-Giles)
  • pillar-top layering work (lit.) (English)
  • pillar-top corbelling (ATTCAT preferred) (English)

Note

    Puzuo 鋪作 that are joined directly into the top of a pillar or column, as opposed to a location on beams between pillars (e.g. 補間鋪作 bujian puzuo).

Related concepts

    鋪作 pūzuò

    斗栱 dòugǒng

    昂 áng

    補間鋪作 bǔjiān pūzuò
