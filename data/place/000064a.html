    • Building Information

    The Longxing Monastery Stone Bridge (Shiqiao 石橋), also called the Triple Path Single Arch Stone Bridge (Sanlu dankong shiqiao 三路單孔石橋) is located in front of the Tianwang Hall (Tianwangdian 天王殿), just across from the “One Word” Glazed Shadow Wall (Yizi liuli yingbi 一字琉璃影壁) located at the southernmost end of the central axis. Although we have not confirmed the date of its first construction, it does appear on a plan of the building from the thirteenth year of the Qianlong reign period (1748; Guo 2009, 369). The name of the bridge comes from the decorative single arch of the bridge and the three paths formed by four rows of balustrades running north and south along the bridge deck.1

    The width of the bridge deck surface is 12.6 meters from east to west, and the horizontal length from north to south is 7.4 meters. The width of the middle lane, measured from the center of the balusters, is 5 meters, and the width of the side lanes is 3.5 meters. Each of the balustrades (goulan 勾欄) is composed of four balusters approximately 1.2 meters in height, which are surmounted by four squatting lions as well as other sculpted components. The lions, two facing south and two facing north, are intricately carved with their heads tilted slightly inward (Hebeisheng Zhengdingxian wenwu baoguansuo 2000, 275).1

    这座三路單孔石橋位於天王殿前，與中軸綫最南端的一字琉璃影壁僅一路相隔。雖然这座石橋還沒有確定其最初建造的日期，它出現在乾隆十三年（1748）的建築平面圖上（郭 2009，369）。橋的名字来源于橋下起裝飾作用的單孔及橋面南北向置勾欄四排形成的三路。1

    橋面的東西寬度為12.6米，南北水平長度為7.4米。中路柱中至柱中的寬度為5米，兩側的寬度為3.5米。勾欄由地栿、望柱、欄板、荷葉凈瓶和尋杖等構件組成。望柱的高度為1.2米。每排有四個雕刻著蹲獅的望柱，南面和北面各有兩個，頭部稍微向內傾斜（河北省正定縣文物保管所 2000，275）。1

    Dynasty Extant from 1748; existence of bridge: Sui - Qing dynasties? 1
