Abstract

ATTCAT is a research forum for the inquiry into the meaning of technical terms in Traditional Chinese Architecture. 2018 was the initial meeting of the forum. ATTCAT 2019 Participants 白颖 BAI Ying (Assistant Professor, School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); 許玉棻 Benda, Yuh-Fen (Asian Studies Librarian, Vanderbilt University, USA); Benda, Christopher (Theological Librarian, Vanderbilt University, USA); 金田 Campbell, Aurelia (Assistant Professor, Morrissey College of Arts and Sciences, Boston College, USA); 陳薇 CHEN Wei (Professor, School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); 陳淑君CHEN Shu-Jiun (Academia Sinica, Taiwan, ROC); 丁垚 DING Yao (Associate Professor, School of Architectural History and Theory, Tianjin University, China); 荷雅麗Harrer, Alexandra (Tsinghua University, China); 胡石 HU Shi (Assistant Professor, School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); 贾亭立 JIA Tingli (Assistant Professor, School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); Johns, Christopher (Professor, College of Arts and Science, Vanderbilt University, USA); 刘妍 LIU Yan (Junior Fellow, SUSTech Society of Fellows in Liberal Arts, Southern University of Science and Technology, China); Lorge, Peter (Associate Professor, College of Arts and Science, Vanderbilt University, USA); 盧履彥LU Lu-Yen (Academia Sinica, Taiwan, ROC); 梅晨曦 Miller, Tracy (Associate Professor, College of Arts and Science, Vanderbilt University, USA); 任思捷 REN Sijie (Assistant Professor, School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); 鲁克思Ruitenbeek, Klass (Asian Art Museum, Berlin, Germany); 孫曉倩SUN Xiaoqian (School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); 唐聪 TANG Cong (Lecturer, Chongqing University, China); 溫靜 WEN Jing (Assistant Professor, Tongji University, China); 俞莉娜YU Lina (Assistant Professor, School of Archeology and Museology, Beijing University, China); 朱光亚Zhu Guangya, (Associate Professor, School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); 诸葛净 ZHUGE Jing (Associate Professor, School of Architecture, Southeast University, China); 左拉拉ZUO Lala (Associate Professor, NYU Shanghai, China)
