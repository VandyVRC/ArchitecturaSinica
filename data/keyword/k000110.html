瓦當 wǎdàng

https://architecturasinica.org/keyword/k000110
Print   TEI/XML  

Terms

  • wadang (Pinyin without tones)
  • 瓦當 (Traditional Chinese)
  • 瓦当 (Simplified Chinese)
  • wǎdàng (Pinyin)
  • wa-tang (Wade-Giles)
  • tile ends (lit.) (English)
  • barrel tile ends (English)1
  • eave-end tile (English)2

Note

    Type of barrel tile (筒瓦 tongwa) used to cap the end of a line of covering tiles at the eaves.

    瓦當的本意是瓦頭.現指 筒瓦 的一種，剖面如 "┍—",作為蓋瓦用於檐頭以遮擋檐部.如明清的 "勾頭".在清代文獻中，"瓦當"似通常專指秦漢等較早時期的瓦當.
