筒瓦 tǒngwǎ

Print   TEI/XML  

Terms

  • tongwa (Pinyin without tones)
  • 筒瓦 (Traditional Chinese)
  • 㼧瓦 (Traditional Chinese)
  • 筒瓦 (Simplified Chinese)
  • tǒngwǎ (Pinyin)
  • t'ong-wa (Wade-Giles)
  • barrel tiles (lit.) (English)1
  • barrel tiles (ATTCAT preferred) (English)

Note

    Barrel tiles. A type of ceramic roofing tile with a semi-circular (or greater) cross section. Usually used as a covering tile to prevent water from seeping between two rows of concave/gutter tiles (banwa 瓪瓦). Also written 㼧 (tong (lit. convex tile)).2

    瓦的一種，也寫作“㼧瓦”。通常是半圓形（或大半圓形），用於屋面作為蓋瓦。
