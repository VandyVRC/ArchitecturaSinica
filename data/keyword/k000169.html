栱 gǒng

Print   TEI/XML  

Terms

  • gong (Pinyin without tones)
  • 栱 (Traditional Chinese)
  • 栱 (Simplified Chinese)
  • gǒng (Pinyin)1
  • kung (Wade-Giles)

Gloss

    bracket arm or arms; one element of a bracket set; the YZFS describes five types: huagong, nidaogong, guazigong, mangong, and linggong (Fu 2017, 356; Chen 2010, 275) 2

    斗栱中的水平向長構件。《營造法式》中常見的五種類型分別為：華栱、泥道栱、瓜子栱、慢栱、令栱。
